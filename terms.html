    3. Cookies

    QuickResults uses cookies to improve your experience on the Platform. Cookies are small pieces of data that a website stores on your device when you visit.

    There are two main types of cookies used on QuickResults:

    Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and are automatically deleted when you close your browser. They are used to remember your preferences and actions within a single browsing session. For example, a session cookie might be used to remember which exam results you were viewing.
    Persistent Cookies: cookies remain on your device for a set period of time or until you manually delete them. They are used to store information about your preferences and actions across multiple browsing sessions. For example, a persistent cookie might be used to remember your login information so you don't have to enter it every time you visit the Platform.

    How QuickResults Uses Cookies
    QuickResults uses cookies for the following purposes:

    To improve your browsing experience Cookies can help us remember your preferences and actions, so you don't have to enter them again the next time you visit the Platform.
    To analyze how you use the Platform Cookies can help us understand how users interact with the Platform, which helps us improve the overall user experience.
    For security purposes: Cookies can help us protect the Platform from fraud and other malicious activity.
